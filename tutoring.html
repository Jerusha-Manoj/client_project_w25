About Tutoring Services

At the University of Michigan School of Information (UMSI), we are committed to supporting students in achieving academic success through a variety of services, including specialized tutoring. Our tutoring services are designed to help students navigate challenging coursework and gain a deeper understanding of complex topics. From one-on-one sessions to group workshops, we provide the resources you need to excel in your studies.

University Library System

UMSI students have access to the University Library system, offering a wide range of digital and physical resources essential for research and coursework. Explore extensive collections, reserve study spaces, and take advantage of research consultations to boost your academic success.
