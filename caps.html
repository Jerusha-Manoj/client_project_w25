Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS)

About CAPS

CAPS (Counseling and Psychological Services) at the University of Michigan is committed to helping students navigate the challenges of university life. CAPS provides confidential mental health and wellness services to all enrolled U-M students to support their academic and personal success.
