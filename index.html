Welcome to the UM Grad Success Navigator

Your Comprehensive Guide to Resources for Graduate School Success

This guide is set up to help you be successul inside and outside of the classroom and we have included the following resources throughout our site:

  1. Academic Resources
  2. Physical Health
  3. Mental Health
  4. Life Outside the Classroom
  5. Contact

Academic Resources

Explore resources that help you thrive academically, including tutoring services and study spaces. Visit our Tutoring Services page for more information.

Physical Health

Maintaining your physical health is crucial for overall success. The Food Pantry page provides information on how to access free, nutritious food to ensure you’re well-nourished and ready for your studies.

Mental Health

Take care of your mental health with services provided by Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS). From individual counseling to urgent crisis support, you can find everything you need on the CAPS page.

Life Outside the Classroom

Graduate school is more than just academics. Explore opportunities to engage with the community, join student organizations, and build a balanced lifestyle. Check out resources for managing life outside of classes, from recreational sports to student organizations.

Contact Us

If you need additional support or have questions about resources, feel free to reach out to UMSI Academic Advising for assistance. Visit the UMSI Academic Advising Website.
